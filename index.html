1. Data We Collect

A. Information You Provide

  • Account & Profile: name, email, role (e.g., student/guardian), profile photo.
  • Support & Feedback: messages you send to us.
  • Emergency Details (if enabled): contact numbers and alert messages.

B. Automatically Collected

  • Device & App Info: device model, OS version, unique identifiers, App version, language, time zone.
  • Usage Data: in-app actions, screens viewed, session duration, performance metrics.
  • Diagnostics: crash logs and error reports (e.g., via Sentry).

C. Location Data (if you allow it)

The App may collect precise location data to provide features such as bus tracking, nearest stop detection, ETA calculation, and emergency alerts. Location may be collected in the foreground and, if enabled, in the background to ensure functionality.

D. Media & Files

Profile images or attachments you upload (stored as URLs on our servers or third‑party storage).

2. How We Use Data

  • Operate and maintain the App (e.g., authentication, profile, notifications).
  • Provide real-time features such as bus location, next stop alerts, and ETA.
  • Monitor performance, analyze usage, and fix bugs.
  • Enhance safety features, including optional emergency alerts.
  • Communicate with you about updates, security notices, and support.
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms.

Legal bases (where applicable): performance of a contract, legitimate interests, consent, and legal obligation.

3. App Permissions

Location

Used to show nearby stops, compute ETAs, and power tracking features. Background access may be requested for continuous functionality.

Notifications

Used for route alerts, ETA updates, emergency messages, and account notifications.

Photos/Media

Used to upload a profile photo or attachments you choose to share.

Network

Required to communicate with our servers and third‑party services.

4. Sharing & Third Parties

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with service providers who help us operate the App. These providers process data on our behalf and under instructions consistent with this Policy.

  • Hosting & Database: {{BACKEND_SERVICES}} (e.g., Firebase/Firestore/Realtime DB, MongoDB, or other managed services).
  • Error & Performance Monitoring: Sentry / sentry-expo.
  • Push Notifications: Expo Push Service / FCM / APNs.
  • Analytics (if enabled): Google Analytics for Firebase or similar tools.

Third‑party policies (examples): Sentry · Firebase · Expo

5. Data Retention

We retain personal information for as long as necessary to provide the App and for legitimate and essential business purposes, such as maintaining performance, making data‑driven decisions, complying with legal obligations, and resolving disputes. We also retain some information in anonymized or aggregated form.

6. Security

We implement technical and organizational measures designed to protect your information. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your region, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict the processing of your personal data, and to object to certain processing or request data portability. You may also withdraw consent at any time where processing is based on consent.

To exercise your rights, contact us at appliedlab@scadpoly.ac.in.

8. Children’s Privacy

The App is intended for use by School and college students. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 (or the equivalent age of consent in your jurisdiction). If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, contact us and we will take appropriate action.

9. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. Where required, we implement appropriate safeguards to protect your data in accordance with applicable laws.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date above and, where appropriate, notify you within the App.
