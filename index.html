1. Data We Collect

A. Information You Provide

  • Account & Profile: name, email, role, profile photo.
  • Support & Feedback: messages you send to us.
  • Emergency Details (if enabled): contact numbers and alert messages.

B. Automatically Collected

  • Device & App Info: device model, OS version, unique identifiers, App version, language, time zone.
  • Usage Data: in-app actions, screens viewed, session duration, performance metrics.
  • Diagnostics: crash logs and error reports.

C. Location Data

The App collects precise location data for bus tracking, ETA, and emergency alerts.

D. Media & Files

Profile images or attachments you upload.

2. How We Use Data

  • Operate and maintain the App
  • Provide real-time features such as bus location and ETA
  • Monitor performance, analyze usage, fix bugs
  • Enhance safety features, including emergency alerts
  • Communicate updates, security notices, and support
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. App Permissions

Location

Used for bus tracking and ETA calculation.

Notifications

Used for alerts and updates.

Photos/Media

Used to upload profile photos.

Network

Required to communicate with servers.

4. Sharing & Third Parties

We do not sell your personal info. We share data only with service providers that operate the App for us.

5. Data Retention

We retain personal information as long as necessary to provide the App and comply with legal obligations. Some data may be anonymized or aggregated.

6. Security

We implement technical and organizational measures to protect your data, but cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Your Rights

You may have rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict processing of your data. Contact us at appliedlab@scadpoly.ac.in.

8. Children’s Privacy

App is intended for school and college students. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

9. International Transfers

Your data may be processed in other countries with safeguards in place.

10. Data Deletion

If you want your personal data removed from Scad Bus:

  1. Visit this page: Data Deletion Instructions
  2. Provide your account info (name, email, phone) or send an email
  3. Email: appliedlab@scadpoly.ac.in
  4. We will delete your personal data within 7 days. Some minimal logs may be retained up to 30 days for security/compliance.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Updates will revise the "Last updated" date and notify users where appropriate.
